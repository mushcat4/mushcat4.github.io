Before You Start: Gilman Website

Firstly, there are two deadlines every year: the first in October and the other in March. You have two opportunities to become a Gilman recipient, so donâ€™t give up! I recommend extensively using the Gilman site for more details on the prompts and what to mention in your essays. I also used this helpful Gilman Essay Outline to help structure my essays.

The essay that you should dedicate the most time to is without a doubt the Statement of Purpose Essay. This is where the reviewers truly get to know you and your motivations for studying abroad. When showing how you overcame a challenging experience, use examples that are unique to you. Emphasize the impact it had on you and how it contributed to your personal growth rather than the event itself. Find ways to highlight the qualities that can connect you to your desired study abroad program.

Working closely with your campus study abroad or scholarship office can help resolve questions you might have about the application process and how to write your essays. I also highly recommend utilizing a writing center to go over and revise your essays before submitting them. I know that these essays are daunting, but if you give yourself enough time to work on them I know you will have an amazing time studying abroad!

Guiding Questions:

Statement of Purpose Essay

  • Do you make a connection between your program and goals?
  • Do you make a connection between your country and your goals?
  • Do you share how you will be academically successful on your program?
  • Do you give examples of your experiences, skills, and knowledge that you will use to meet program challenges?
  • Do you address how this abroad experience will impact your future?

Building Mutual Understanding Essay

  • Do you articulate how you will represent the United States as a citizen diplomat while abroad?
  • Do you explain how you will contribute to the goal of building mutual understanding?
  • Do you discuss how you will look for opportunities to build meaningful relationships and become more culturally engaged?

Follow-on Service Project Proposal

  • Does your FOSP increase awareness of the Gilman Scholarship and study abroad?
  • Is your project feasible?
  • Do you give a detailed plan for your project?
  • Do you include your study abroad experience into your project?
  • Do you have an intended audience? Who will you collaborate with to reach this community?
